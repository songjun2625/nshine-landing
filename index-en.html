FAQ

The practical questions.

Will Dr. Cho personally perform the treatment?

Yes. The N Shine model is built on it: Dr. Cho personally reviews your virtual consult, conducts your in-person diagnosis, performs every treatment, and handles your post-care follow-up. This is the difference from typical large-volume clinics.

How many days should I plan to stay in Seoul?

Single non-invasive treatments (Ultherapy, Thermage, ONDA): 2 nights. Combination protocols: 3 nights. Thread lifting: 4–5 nights to accommodate initial swelling. We provide a tailored itinerary in your pre-arrival plan.

How does pricing work? Are there hidden fees?

You receive a written treatment quote in USD before booking your flight. The quote is honored at consultation. We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and Apple Pay — no facility fees, no after-the-fact charges.

Will the language be an issue?

No. International patient coordinators speak fluent English. Every consent form, treatment plan, and aftercare document is provided in English, and a coordinator stays with you throughout your treatment day.

What if I have a concern after I fly home?

You'll have direct WhatsApp access for 90 days post-treatment, with scheduled video check-ins at week 2, 4, and 8. If a concern requires in-person assessment, we'll arrange a complimentary follow-up visit (travel not included).
